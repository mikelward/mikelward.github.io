I just bought an Epson TX100 multifunction printer.

Epson TX100



The brochure claims up to 14 pages per minute black and 26 pages per minute colour.

Printing a web page with a small amount of coloured text and no images takes over a minute.

You can change to draft mode, which takes about ten seconds, but the output is so poor that you wouldn't bother using it.

I'm very disappointed and wouldn't recommend this printer.
